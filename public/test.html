The forecast calls for Snow Flurry™!

Easy to get on and off, with 200 grams of insulation to keep them on their toes in the snow. We add insulation under foot (where heat escapes) to ensure their toes stay toasty. Featuring our unique OpenWIDE® construction designed to extend twice as wide so feet can easily slip inside. Plus, reflective tape for added safety and an interior name label so their boots won’t walk off on their own.


Features

  • Water-resistant, man-made upper
  • 200-gram Thermolite® insulation
  • Super easy OpenWIDE slip-on design
  • Antimicrobial finish reduces shoe odor
  • Rip grip side closures
  • Interior name label
  • Spot clean
  • Imported
  • Kids’ feet grow fast. Find the right size with our printable foot ruler

My kids wore these during a family snow trip to Colorado. We're from Texas, so not much experience with snow and I did a ton of research on boots for the kids before settling on these from Lands End. I"m so glad I did! They were SUPER warm - never any complaints about cold feet, despite being out in the snow ~8 hours each day. I bought the boots 1/2 size up from the shoe size my kids are in. The fit was great. We wore them with thick, smartwool socks - plenty of room and socks were bone dry at the end of the day. I read a few reviews saying kids feet were wet?? That was NOT our experience. My kids rolled, romped and played in the snow like crazy, so rest assured these boots ARE WATERPROOF and will keep your kiddos feet dry! They were VERY easy to get on and off. Though with the wide mouth of the boot, my kids needed help tucking in their snow bibs, sealing the boot, and then ensuring the outer leg of the bib was outside the boot. I would recommend this boot to anyone - well worth the price to ensure everyone is comfortable and enjoys the winter fun!
